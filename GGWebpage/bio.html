Gia Gonzales is a poet based in New York City, where she was born. She is the author of the chaplet Gia Gonzales (Belladonna* Collaborative) and the chapbook RENDER SLEAZE (Portable Press @ Yo-Yo Labs). From 2023–2024 she was an Emerge-Surface-Be fellow at the Poetry Project. Currently, she is an editor at large for Nightboat Books.


(Gia is pronounced “Ghee-uh”)
